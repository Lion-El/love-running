why love running with us?

feel healthy

Since it improves aerobic fitness, running is a great way to improve your cardiovascular health. Plus it builds muscle and can build strength.

feel strong

Running strengthens your heart by pumping blood more effectivly through the body.

feel free

Physical excercise, running included is known to release endorphins in the brain. Endorfins make you more energetic, more awake and yes, happier.

get social

When we can run with others we can run farther and faster more easily. Social running can pull you out of a running funk or low mood, and inspire you to try new events.
